Renewable Energy for Food & Water Engineering

Rooted in Tradition, Powered by Innovation.

Inspiring growth, fostering sustainability.

With a foundation built on sustainable farming and innovation, REFWE brings together the best of agriculture and renewable energy to cultivate a brighter future. From nurturing diverse crops and livestock to harnessing solar power across every aspect of our operations, we believe in transparency, resilience, and respect for the land. Our commitment to sustainability not only enriches our community but also ensures a healthier world for the generations to come.

Harvesting Health, Naturally

Our dedication to fresh produce and livestock lies in every field and barn. From nutrient-rich vegetables to premium poultry and cattle.

We focus on sustainable methods to deliver wholesome products straight from our farm to your plate.

Adding Value, Naturally

With a commitment to zero waste, we extend our produce’s life through innovative processing methods. From drying fruits to milling grains, our value addition processes create quality products that nourish and delight.

Efficiency Meets Innovation

Empowering our farm with advanced machinery enables us to boost productivity while preserving quality. From solar dryers to size sorting machines, each piece of equipment is chosen to help us produce efficiently and sustainably.

Powered by the Sun

Harnessing the sun’s power not only fuels our crops but also drives our mission for sustainable farming. Our solar-powered solutions, from lighting to cooling systems, help us operate with low environmental impact.

Maize

Our maize is harvested with care, delivering fresh, high-quality grains that serve as a staple for nutritious meals and versatile culinary uses.

Dried Mushrooms

Earthy and nutrient-dense, our packed dried mushrooms bring a robust, savory flavor to your dishes. Perfect for soups, stews, and sauces, they deliver the essence of fresh mushrooms with the convenience of long-lasting storage.

Cow Ghee

Crafted from premium dairy, our cow ghee brings a rich, authentic flavor to your kitchen, ideal for cooking, baking, or enhancing your favorite recipes.

About us

Our farm has been a cornerstone of our family for over 20 years, built on a foundation of hard work, passion, and dedication. Recently, we took the next step in expanding our vision by establishing REFWE Company, which is proudly run by our family. We continue to honor our roots by combining time-honored farming practices with modern techniques to grow high-quality produce and provide sustainable solutions.
