Greetings and Welcome to REFWE Co. Ltd. As of 2025, we are a four-year-old company in Uganda doing an Engineering based, and Renewable Energy-powered, Value Adding and Transformational Agrobusiness. In the first two and half years, we were setting ourselves up in form of required infrastructure. In our business, we seek to create Health, Wealth and Transformation for ourselves and for all those who interact with our products and services. We run a unique elite business rooted deep in Isanga village, Kyegegwa district - a Uganda rural area with neither grid electricity nor running water nor skilled personnel nor good public-supplied other infrastructure. We thus use our own ingenuity to provide these key industrializing factors to ourselves and some, to the surrounding community: thanks to the engineering background and/or engineering-tuning of all the directors of the company and the fast learning of key workers we take on. Here below, I briefly explain what we do, how we do it and why you should be doing business with us. 1. Mixed Farming – On small pieces of land, we necessarily do crop and animal production round the year. This helps reduce waste (with engineering, we virtually eliminate agro-waste even in extreme cases of nature-induced failures) from either of two husbandry activities, since each supports the other. This eliminates many business-cost elements and enables us to sell ecologically sound products both from animal and plant sources at extremely competitive prices. For top quality agro-products we supply to regular customers, we are thus a reliable and most cost-effective supplier. 2. Engineering Products and Services – We design (on some, we innovate or even invent), manufacture and install key agricultural and other renewable energy-consuming thermo-electro-mechanical systems. For invented systems such as special dryers, sun-tracking solar water heaters, industrial bio-mass heating and drying systems etc., we do not sell them out until modular units have been tested in our own operations. Therefore, when you buy these systems, not only are you assured of functionality and fast manufacture/installation, but you also get additional post acquisition support. And all this, you get at a minute fraction of the cost you would incur if you were to import perhaps even inappropriately sized or styled substitutes. 3. Research, Development and Training – Ours is in many respects, a R&D and training unit: For we are tackling problems that other normal businesspeople prefer to relegate to elsewhere. For example, we seek total independence from outside on energy, water and food, for ourselves, our animals, plants and fungi. We train village youngsters as early as pre-primary in scientific ways of addressing both nature and man – imposed obstacles to progress; we equip willing adults with basic artisanal skills to solve everyday problems not only at our farm but also in their homes, etc., etc. Most important, we research on selected society problems, formulate engineering solutions to them, create and grow local markets for the solutions – and thus contribute to positive transformation. Therefore, unlike other businesses, we do not rely only on already existing markets. We value creation of new local, regional and continental ones a lot more. Therefore, when you work at our farm, workshop or micro factories, you become better skilled, more enlightened and a real positive change agent to society at large. When you do business with us, you directly contribute to the transformational process of moving our society from peasantry to industrial: a very liberating and long-sought objective in Africa. Finally, I thank you once again for your precious time visiting our website. On different pages, you will find what is available at REFWE. This will keep evolving – as ours is essentially a continuously innovating company. Keep checking, and most importantly, find something to do with us. Cheers – and stay blessed.

Kant Kanyarusoke
President & CEO
